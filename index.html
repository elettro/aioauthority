Frequently Asked Questions

What is AI Search Optimization?

AIO focuses on structuring your web presence so that Large Language Models (LLMs) easily understand and recommend your brand.

How do Voice Agents work?

Our agents are trained on your specific business data to handle calls naturally, 24/7, with human-like latency.

What is Entity Schema Injection?

It is a process of embedding advanced structured data into your site code to define your business as a unique, authoritative entity in the global knowledge graph.

Do I need to replace my existing SEO team?

Not necessarily. We often work alongside traditional SEO teams to provide the specialized AI-layer optimization that standard agencies aren't yet equipped for.

How does Semantic Search affect my rankings?

Semantic search understands intent and context. By optimizing for semantics, you ensure your business is found for concepts, not just keywords.

Is AI Optimization only for Google?

No. Our strategies optimize your visibility across the entire AI ecosystem, including Perplexity, ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini.

How does AI production differ from traditional digital content creation?

AI production focuses on building structured, entity-based content that is specifically designed for neural network interpretation, whereas traditional content is often just for human readability.

What is 'Search Intelligence' in the context of AI?

Search Intelligence is the analysis of how AI models like GPT-4, Claude, and Gemini perceive and rank your brand based on available structured data and web-wide entity signals.

How long does it take to see results from AI optimization?

While initial technical indexing is fast, establishing strong authority across AI discovery platforms typically takes 3-6 months as models update their knowledge graphs.

Can AI optimization help with crisis management or brand reputation?

Yes. By providing clear, structured, and factual data directly to AI systems, you can ensure that AI-generated summaries about your brand are accurate and representative of your true messaging.
