Frequently Asked Questions

What is AI Search Optimization?

AIO focuses on structuring your web presence so that Large Language Models (LLMs) easily understand and recommend your brand in generative search results.

Does my website need to be built by you to get AI Optimization?

No — we can apply schema injection, entity optimization, and semantic restructuring to your existing site. That said, sites we build from scratch have the advantage of AI-readiness baked in from day one, which produces faster results.

What is the connection between AIO Authority and Elettro?

AIO Authority is the dedicated AI optimization division of Elettro® — a full-service creative production studio. Clients benefit from Elettro's broader capabilities in branding, web development, Google Ads, and marketing automation, with AIO Authority providing the specialized AI search layer on top.

How do Voice Agents work?

Our agents are trained on your specific business data to handle calls naturally, 24/7, with human-like latency. They integrate with your CRM and calendar systems for appointment booking and lead capture.

What is Entity Schema Injection?

A process of embedding advanced structured data into your site code to define your business as a unique, authoritative entity in the global knowledge graph — making it unambiguous to AI systems who you are and what you do.

Do I need to replace my existing SEO team?

Not necessarily. We often work alongside traditional SEO teams to provide the specialized AI-layer optimization that standard agencies aren't yet equipped for.

Is AI Optimization only for Google?

No. Our strategies optimize your visibility across the entire AI ecosystem — including Perplexity, ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini — not just traditional Google search.

How long does it take to see results?

Initial technical indexing is fast, but establishing strong authority across AI discovery platforms typically takes 3–6 months as models update their knowledge graphs.

Can AI optimization help with brand reputation?

Yes. By providing clear, structured, and factual data directly to AI systems, you ensure that AI-generated summaries about your brand are accurate and representative of your true messaging.
